Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Overview

Qalb ("we", "our") is built as a minimalist Islamic companion. We value your privacy and aim for total transparency.

2. Data Collection (Geolocation)

Our app requests access to your Approximate Location. This data is used solely to calculate accurate local prayer timings. This calculation is performed on your device or via a secure API call. We do not store or share your location history on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Aladhan API and Alquran Cloud to provide prayer and Quranic data. These services receive coordinates to provide timings but do not receive personally identifiable information.

4. Local Storage

Preferences such as bookmarks and cached prayer times are stored locally on your device (LocalStorage). These stay on your device unless you clear your app data.

5. Contact

For questions regarding privacy, contact us via our GitHub repository or developer profile.
